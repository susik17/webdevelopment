Favourite Places

Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple (Srirangam)

A must-visit, this temple is one of the largest in India and an important pilgrimage site for Hindus. Its intricate architecture and vast size are awe-inspiring.

Rockfort Temple

This iconic temple, perched atop a rocky hill, offers a stunning view of the city. The climb to the temple is a bit challenging,but more attractive.

Jambukeswarar Temple

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this temple is a significant religious site in Trichy. The temple is famous for the water element present in its sanctum, symbolizing the flow of water.

Kallanai Dam (Grand Anicut)

Located near Trichy, this ancient dam built by the Chola kings is one of the oldest in the world. The surrounding area is picturesque and a great place for a peaceful outing.
